How it works

three steps. one paste.

no timeline to scrub, no keyframes to set. paste a url, pick a look, ship the video.

01

paste.

drop your landing url. pyrocut opens the page, reads the brand — palette, type, the hero copy — and pulls your real screens. no setup, no upload dance.

02

direct.

add a screenshot or a line of brief if you want, then pick one of five looks and a format. pyrocut storyboards the beats and times every move.

03

ship.

preview it live in the browser, then render a 1080p mp4 in 16:9 or 9:16 — or grab a share link and paste it straight into the launch post you already had open.

Questions

the fine print.

what do you actually need from me?
just a public url. pyrocut opens your landing, reads the brand and pulls real screens on its own. screenshots and a one-line brief are optional — they only make the cut sharper.
will it actually match my brand?
yes. it extracts your palette, fonts and hero copy from the page and carries them across the whole video — backdrop, type and accents. you review the brand before it composes, so you can fix a swatch if the auto-pick is off.
how long does a video take?
you preview the composition live in the browser while it builds, then it renders in the background — progress shows on a scrubber, not a spinner. no render farm to queue behind.
what do i get to download?
a 1080p mp4 in 16:9 or 9:16. you preview it live first, then download the file or hand someone a private share link.
can i use my own screenshots?
yes. drop in screens or a short brief and pyrocut works them into the storyboard instead of — or alongside — the ones it pulls from your url.
is my site data safe?
pyrocut only reads what's publicly on your landing page. nothing is kept beyond the screens it needs to render, and you can delete a project any time.
