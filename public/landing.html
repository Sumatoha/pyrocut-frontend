questions

the fine print.

what do you actually need from me?
just a public url. pyrocut opens your landing, reads the brand and pulls real screens on its own. a one-line brief and your own screenshots are optional — they only make the cut sharper.
will it actually match my brand?
yes. it extracts your palette, fonts and hero copy from the page and carries them across the whole reel — backdrop, type and accents. you review the brand before it composes, so you can fix a swatch if the auto-pick is off.
what do i get to download?
a 1080p mp4 in 16:9 or 9:16. you preview it live in the browser first, then download the file or hand someone a private share link.
how many looks are there?
eight — dolly, snapcut, editorial, neon, kinetic, glass, terminal and liquid. each is a different rhythm and runs 13–22 seconds. the colors and mood come from your page, so the same look on two sites still feels distinct.
can i use my own screenshots?
yes. drop in screens or a short brief and pyrocut works them into the storyboard, alongside the ones it pulls from your url.
is my site data safe?
pyrocut only reads what's publicly on your landing page. nothing is kept beyond the screens it needs to compose the reel, and you can delete a project any time.
